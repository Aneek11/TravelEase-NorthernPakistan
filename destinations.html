Top Destinations of Northern Pakistan

Gilgit-Baltistan

Hunza

Known for Altit Fort, Baltit Fort, Attabad Lake, and apricot orchards.
Best Months: May – October
Approx. Cost: PKR 25,000 – 40,000

Skardu

Famous for Shangrila Resort, Upper Kachura Lake, Deosai Plains.
Best Months: June – September
Approx. Cost: PKR 30,000 – 50,000

Khaplu

Historic Khaplu Palace and Mesmerizing Valleys.
Best Months: May – September
Approx. Cost: PKR 20,000 – 35,000

Fairy Meadows

Gateway to Nanga Parbat base camp, one of the most scenic spots in Pakistan.
Best Months: June – September
Approx. Cost: PKR 15,000 – 30,000

Khyber Pakhtunkhwa

Swat

Known as the "Switzerland of the East". Mingora, Malam Jabba ski resort.
Best Months: April – October
Approx. Cost: PKR 20,000 – 35,000

Kalam

Beautiful Ushu Forest, Mahodand Lake.
Best Months: May – September
Approx. Cost: PKR 18,000 – 30,000

Malam Jabba

Famous for ski resort and chairlifts.
Best Months: December – February (snow lovers)
Approx. Cost: PKR 15,000 – 25,000

Naran & Kaghan

Saif-ul-Malook Lake, Lulusar Lake, Babusar Top.
Best Months: May – September
Approx. Cost: PKR 25,000 – 40,000
